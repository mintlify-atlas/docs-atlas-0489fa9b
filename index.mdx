# Welcome to B-Accesible

> *"La banca que te acompaña. Tecnología que te entiende."*

**B-Accesible** is an accessible digital banking proof-of-concept designed for everyone — especially people with low digital literacy and visual, auditory, or cognitive disabilities. It uses responsible AI to adapt the interface to each user's needs.
